SHORT FILM CREATION

When an ancient general picked up a mobile phone from the future, who was the hero? Who made history again?

【Function Description】I participated in the creation of this short film, and was mainly responsible for art props, lighting, sound recording and post-production; proposed an idea in the script adaptation which was adopted by the crew, i.e. "alternate the period setting and integrating universal values and emotions into the localized scenes and narratives"; modified the details of the split-screen script to fit the period scenes. In mid-shoot, we worked with the lighting plan, focusing on light and shadow to show the emotional changes of the main character. In post-production, I repaired the mismatched scenes, replaced the sky, simulated light and fire, created special effects, and did editing montages to create various coincidences to achieve the final hero; finally, in the sound effects, I enhanced the emotional ups and downs of the characters through natural sound background music.






THE GENERAL'S MOBILE PHONE
When a rabbit can't get rid of fur after cultivating herself as a human being for true love, what does she end up using to hold on to her beauty?

【Function Description】I was the writer and director of this short film, focusing on the presentation of the product's selling points and the communication of the brand level; by deconstructing the story elements of the Chinese mythological character Rabbit Fairy and integrating them into the aesthetics of modern society, the character's pain points were paved out to subtly promote the modern hair removal cream product in a classical form, and with the point of clarity to sublimate the core of the product "Beauty, should be unreserved "It is easy to create a visual effect and also strikes a balance between creative promotion and the public viewing experience through the grafting and appropriation of different narrative genres and misplaced character relationships. With a light-hearted and witty style and tone, this commercial uses romantic love as the core of the story development, which is in line with the psychological and demand pain points of the main female audience group of this brand of hair removal cream, and also matches the brand concept.

CREATIVE ADVERTISEMENT FOR HAIR REMOVAL CREAM
When a teacher sentenced to 15 years in prison is released, what was once there is long gone ...

【Function Description】I was the writer and director of this short film, focusing on movement shots and changes in camera position to highlight the main character's unfamiliarity and sadness with today's society. The film begins with close-up follow-up shots such as foot shots to create a sense of mystery for the main character's image and to build momentum for the next shot that pans to the main character's facial monologue. When the protagonist returns to his old school, a low angle shot of the ground is used to add layers and movement to the image, followed by a pull-out shot showing a panoramic view of the protagonist's inner loneliness and sense of unreality; in addition to the design of the shot, the visual effect of light and shadow on the protagonist's face is also used to render the emotional ups and downs of the protagonist, and the increasing shadows on the protagonist's face add a tragic tone to the whole film.





RELEASED FROM PRISON
Love is about wanting to touch but retracting your hand, but love is also about falling from here and rising from there.

【Function Description】I wrote and directed this short film, which is based on an award-winning short film, with a focus on parallel montage editing. The witty reversals add comic effect, and the alternating episodes of letter-writing and reminiscing make the psychological characterization of the characters richer and fuller; the whole process is substituted into the protagonist's point of view, and the recurring montage of the letter-writing episode appears several times, which keeps advancing the plot and makes the protagonist's change of heart more surprising to the audience.

A MIDSUMMER NIGHT
He was diagnosed as a paralyzed patient since he was a child. Now he is also the only Chinese medicine doctor in the town. People call him the Chinese medicine on the bench.

【Function Description】I took on the writing, lighting and music duties for this documentary, based on Dr. Pang's autobiography, uncovering the highlights and planning and writing the overall documentary. I adopted a low and flat perspective, leaving aside the high tone, and interweaving the bright and dark threads to drive the narrative events. Through daily records and in-depth interviews, Dr Pang's positive and optimistic attitude towards life is shown, but even more touching is his mother, who has always supported and been there for him.
