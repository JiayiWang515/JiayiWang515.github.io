    "In the Heart of the Sun"Film Appreciation

    ——Analysis of movie aside

    aside occupies an important position in the film As one of the three elements of sound in film and television art, human voice is the most direct way to convey the emotions of the characters and the plot of the movie to the audience, and it is also the easiest way to be accepted by the public. Character language is the most important part of the human voice. In the process of character expression, different language content, pitch, timbre, rhythm, and intensity convey the character, mood, and temperament of the character, and promote the development of the plot. The duration of a movie is usually about two hours. It is not easy to complete a grand narrative that spans multiple time spaces in this short period of time. Therefore, aside is extremely important.

    In the movie "In the Heart of the Sun", aside plays a major role in two aspects.

    On the one hand, aside explains the story background of the entire film, while condensing events and time, highlighting the theme of the story, and giving the picture a deeper meaning. At the beginning of the movie, as the cast and crew members flashed one by one, Actor Jiang Wen’s low-pitched, unique aside introduces the audience into the plot, “Beijing has become so fast, it has become a modern city in twenty years, and I can hardly find anything in my memory. In fact, this change has destroyed my memory, making me unable to distinguish between illusion and reality. My story always happens in summer..."

    The aside in this part is also one of the clever uses of the blank art of the film. It does not directly tell you the changes in Beijing. Instead, it gives people unlimited reverie—what was Beijing like at that time? What happened in the summer in Beijing? As the aside sound fades away, the movie enters the subject.

    Another function of aside is to express the thoughts and feelings of the characters. Film art can convey the external world of the task through the picture, such as the expressions, actions, and dialogues of the characters. The performance of the character's inner world should be more subtle and introverted. The aside of the character makes up for this. It can show the complex thoughts and feelings of the character.

    This is particularly important in "In the Heart of the Sun", and it has already been seen from the beginning of the story introduction. Then, as the movie screen entered the stage before the soldiers went out, Aside’s narrative also unfolds with the content of the screen, showing the protagonist’s ignorant fantasy when he was young, "My biggest fantasy is the Sino-Soviet war, because I firmly believe that in a new world war, The iron fist of our army will surely smash the war machine of the Soviet Union and the United States. A war hero that attracts worldwide attention will be born from this, and that is me. "

    The tone of the narrator becomes powerful at this time, and every word is very firm. This is Ma Xiaojun’s youth fantasy, vividly showing his idolatry and desire to become a hero. Such an ignorant and fearless vision is in line with the characteristics of Chinese society during the Cultural Revolution. At the same time, this aside also takes over the following content. It brings a natural transition to the characters and plot of the film. Ma Xiaojun, a child full of illusions about the future, quickly grows into a rebellious teenager.

    The role of aside is not single, it not only makes the film narrative more smooth and delicate, but also gives the audience a chance to see the inner world of the movie characters and the plot hidden behind the appearance. The film art also appears more hierarchical due to the rational use of aside, and its connotation is also richer.
